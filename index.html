About me

Angelo Paolo Santos is a full stack web developer. He strives to build intuitive, clean and beautiful web applications by following coding standards and focusing on user experience. He truly believes that simple is beautiful.

After graduating with a B.S. in Computer Engineering from Mapua Institute of Technology in 2005, Angelo has been building his professional career in the I.T. industry.

He works both as an I.T. Manager and Web Developer. With a deep passion for coding, he continually studies web technologies such as React, Angular, Sass, Mongo, Express, Webpack, and Wordpress.

When not in front of a computer, Angelo can be found either travelling the world with his wife and daughter, or at home in Torrance, California playing video games.
